Fargo

Fargo is an interesting city with a lot of neat things going on!

  1. Fargo has an an extreme climate due to it's geographical location
  2. Fargo was featured in the academy award winning black comedy "Fargo", however this movie was actually filmed primarily in Minnesota
  3. Fargo has a lot of fun outdoor activities for the whole family, both winter and summer activities alike!

Come to Fargo to enjoy exciting things such as

  • Many great breweries
  • Agriculture and outdoor activities such as hiking or fishing
  • Winter activities such as skiing or sledding
  • You are a movie buff!
